SPEC'S AND REVIEW OF SAMSUNG GALAXY A10 2019

Simple design in living color

Galaxy A10's slender 7.9 mm body feels like it's made for your hand. The glossy plastic finish stands out all on its own, and you can choose from black, blue, or red. Once it's in your grip, you'll never let go.

A camera to capture the moment

Take photos the way you want with the 13MP rear and 5MP front selfie camera for picture-book snapshots. Your memories stay vivid, bright, and clear with the tap of a button. And the Octa Core processor makes everything run smoothly to keep up with how you live life.

Enjoy your photos to the fullest

A photo isn't just a photo with Galaxy A10 camera. Decorate your memories with AR stickers, stamps, and filters to continue the fun even after you nail the shot.

Power for your everyday

Galaxy A10's 3,400 mAh (typical)* battery is enough for your day-to-day. Whether you're the type to have texting marathons with your friends, watch livestream, or do what you love on a phone that lasts.

Look to unlock

Samsung Galaxy A10 gives you personalized protection with face recognition technology. It knows you, so you can simply hold the phone to your face for an easy, secure way to unlock and access. It's that simple.

Made to focus

One UI helps you focus on what matters. The hardware and software work in harmony to intuitively deliver content and features at your fingertips. Night Mode helps you wind down your day as it turns to a dark mode to go easy on eyes for a better nights rest.
